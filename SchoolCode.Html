WASTE TO
WELLNESS
  • By using wheat straw and rice waste, we reduce the amount of agricultural residue, minimizing the need for disposal and decreasing pollution. Utilizing these materials in eco-friendly products can help sequester carbon from the atmosphere, contributing to climate change mitigation. They can be used to make biodegradable and compostable packaging products, reducing plastic waste.

  • Wheat straw and rice waste can be utilized as livestock feed, ensuring efficient use of resources in the agricultural industry. Wheat Straw when mixed with other feed ingredients or supplemented with protein-rich sources, it can serve as a filler or roughage to support the digestive system of ruminants. Similar to wheat straw, rice waste is primarily used as roughage for ruminant animals.

  • Maize cobs can be used for crafting purposes, reducing the demand for other materials and promoting eco-friendly creativity. It can be utilized as natural garden decorations, adding a rustic touch without contributing to environmental harm. Additionally, using these waste materials as animal feed can help reduce food waste and promote a more sustainable agricultural system.
